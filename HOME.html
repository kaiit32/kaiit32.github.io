Flamenco Ice Tower

An international team of Dutch and Chinese students and professors from Eindhoven University of
Technology (TU/e), Summa College, the Netherlands, and Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT),
China, realized the largest ice shell ever after two years of preparation. On 10 January the tower
will be opened by the Dutch ambassador in China.

Projects Timeline

Our research started in 2003 when the first indoor igloo was built. From 2014 till 2017, we have built the world
record for building the largest ice dome,the highest ice dome, Flamenco Ice Tower, Sagrada Familia in ice
and bridge in ice. This year, we are going to work on a 3D printed grid shell in ice at Harbin Institute
of Technology, China. Furthermore, our future plan is to build the ice house on Mars.

International Joint Workshop Harbin 2017

Students have to design a mega large polyhydric ice structure.
The structure will be made by freezing a cable structure at the
inside of an inflatable. After the cable structure can stand on it
selve the inflatbable support structure at the outside can be
removed en reused for new structures.

Bridge in ice

A pedestrian bridge in wood based on Leonardo’s design has been built in Norway, as well as some small ice bridges. In the winter of 2016 a scale model of this bridge with a span of 50 meters will be created in ice in Finland. It will be the biggest single-span structure in ice ever realized.

Read More

Pykrete Dome

By realizing the Pykrete Dome project in January 2014, the world record for building the largest ice dome was set. The span of the dome was 30 meters, 5 meters more than the standing record at that time, which was held by T. Kokawa.

Read More

Sagrada Familia in Ice

The goal of the project is to realize an ice structure which is based on the design of the Sagrada Familia in Barcelona. The design will consist of several domes. One dome will have a height of 30 meters, which will be the highest ice dome in the world.
