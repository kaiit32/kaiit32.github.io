Construction Daily News

We are under construction process now. The students from TU/e, Summa and HIT are working in the extreme cold,
the construction works is going slowly and tough under minus 25 degree in Harbin, but our students will overcome
all the difficulties and persist to achieve our goals.

Professors arrives Dec.14th

All the professors from TUE and KUL were arrived yesterday.We had a short meeting on site, explained all the progress of our Ice Tower. Though the weather is getting colder, we still have high enthusiasm to work!

Container Arrives Dec.13th

Our container arrived! After thousands of kilometers by train our baby arrived on the building site! We are so happy!

Ice Ring Foundation Dec.13th

Today, we start to construct our Ice Tower!It is super cold here in Harbin, -25℃！The first step today is to settle the ice foundation, which will be the stairs to go inside of tower later. A single ice block is super heavy, we learn some skills from the ice builder to place and shape the ice block!
